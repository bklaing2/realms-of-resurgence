# Description
Small ancient coin shaped piece of weak metal.

When crushed over a [[Rune]], it will immediately ruin the circle and cancel the active rune.

The creature who crushes it immediately takes 1d6 damage and bleeds from the nose and until the next long rest is fatigued and has a -10 ft to any movement speed.
