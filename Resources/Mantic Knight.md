[[The Wastes]] 
**AC**: 15  
**HP**: 30  
**Speed**: 30 ft  
**Finesse**: +4  
**Mantic**: +4  
**Perception**: +1

**Passives**:  
**Mantic Armor**: When the knight is attacked by melee, the creature takes 1d4 mantic damage

**Actions**:  
**Multiattack**: (2) Mantic Knight makes 2 attacks (longsword and punch) each turn.

# Attacks:
## Longsword:
+4 to hit  
5 ft range  
2d8 + 4 slashing damage

## Mantic Punch:
+6 to hit  
5 ft range  
2d6 + 4 mantic damage

# Proximity Radical Fail Outcome
When a PC within 15 ft rolls a radiation failure, the Mantic Knight's armor fills with mantic energy and, at the start of it's next turn, a blast of mantic energy shoots out in a 15 ft cone. PCs in the cone make a DC 13 Finesse saving throw or take 5d6 mantic damage and 1/2 on a success.
