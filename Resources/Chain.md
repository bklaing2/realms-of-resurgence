# Description

As an action, target a creature within 30ft. The creature taker a d4 of damage. It then jumps to another creature within 15ft of it. On a successful hit it damages that creature and jumps to another in range that hasn't been hit by the spell this turn.


# Radiation Fail Outcome

It targets to the closest creature to you, or you if there's no one else within 30ft.
