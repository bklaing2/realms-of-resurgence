# Description

As a bonus action, begin gathering Mantic energy around a single point near yourself. Use an action to either throw it within a range of 30ft or explode it centered on yourself. Each turn you spend your bonus action focusing on gathering Neomancy increases the damage done by the spell by 1D6 up to a max of 5D6. The spell must be used on the turn it reaches max charge.

# Antimantic Inverse

As a bonus action, begin gathering Mantic energy around a single point near yourself. Use an action to either throw it within a range of 30ft or explode it centered on yourself. Each turn you spend your bonus action focusing on gathering Neomancy increases the healing done by the spell by 1D6 up to a max of 5D6. The spell must be used on the turn it reaches max charge.
