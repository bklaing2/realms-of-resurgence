# [[Character Creation|Class]] Abilities

## Passive: Fortified

Add your [[Fortitude]] when rolling hp while leveling up

## Passive: Intimidate

After a succeeding on an [[intimidation]] check against a creature, the creature is optionally frightened for 1 round
At level 5 it lasts 2 rounds

## Frenzy

Bonus action to enter a Frenzy.
Take half damage from bludgeoning, slashing, and piercing damage while in a Frenzy. Add extra damage equal to the Frenzy Damage found on the level table. Frenzy lasts 1 minute.
