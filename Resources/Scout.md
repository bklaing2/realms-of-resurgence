# Abilities

## Passive: Stealth

Less detectable by creatures
A level 5, hunters are unaffected by nonmagical difficult terrain

## Hide

Use an action to hide in shadows, rendering you virtually invisible. Attacks while hidden gain advantage, but you are no longer hidden. Can craft [[Arrows#Tier One|tier one arrows]].

## Hunter's Mark

As a bonus action, mark a creature. Every attack against the creature gains advantage. When the creature dies the mark can be moved to another creature with a subsequent bonus action.
Can craft [[Arrows#Tier Two|tier two arrows]].

# Other

Hunters have a limited number of arrows and need to go back an collect them
Hunter's gear. Includes a mirror for signaling from afar.
