# Proximity Radical Fail Outcome

When a PC within 15 ft rolls a [[Radical Fail]], the Mutated Exiled spews bodily fluids from its mouth in a 15 ft cone, dealing 1d10 +3 [[Poison (Damage)]] damage to anyone who fails a DC 13 [[Finesse]] saving throw. Half damage on a success.

# Description

An [[Exiled]] who was cast out of a Settlement into [[The Wastes]], and has subsequently been mutated.
