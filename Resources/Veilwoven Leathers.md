# Description
Stitched with threads taken from ancient rune-covered banners.

Can be only be [[Light Armor]]
Same stats of light, but base AC and the damage threshold are increased by 1.

Once a short rest, allows the user to use a reaction to phase to one side by 5 ft, causing an attack that was going to hit to miss.

Once in a blue moon, will phase the wearer 30 ft in a random direction.

Blurs the wearer slightly at a distance.
