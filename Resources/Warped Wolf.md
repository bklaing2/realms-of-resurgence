[[The Wastes]], [[Components]]
**AC**: 15
**HP**: 12
**Speed**: 45 ft
**[[Fortitude]]**: +6
**[[Mantic]]**: +3
**Perception**: +5

**Passives**:
**Keen hearing and smell**: Advantage on perception checks using hearing and smell

**Actions**:
**Multiattack**: (2) Warped wolf makes 2 attacks (claw and bite) each turn. (Becomes 3 after a PC radiation fail)
# Attacks:

## Claw: 
+6 to hit
5 ft range
2d6 + 6 slashing damage
## Bite:
+6 to hit
5 ft range
4d4 + 6 piercing damage

## Mantacle:
+9 to hit
10 ft range
4d6 + 3 mantic damage
Target must make a DC 13 [[Fortitude]] saving throw or be restrained, and the wolf cannot make another mantacle attack while a PC is restrained in this way.

# Proximity Radical Fail Outcome

When a PC within 15 ft rolls a radiation failure, the Warped Wolf immediately rips open their body more and the mantacles extend. (Unlocks Mantacle attack)
