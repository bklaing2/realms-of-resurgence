# Abilities
## Passive: Frighten
When they succeed an intimidation check against a creature, the creature is frightened for 1 round
At level 5 it lasts 2 rounds

## Mania
Bonus action to enter a Mania.
Take half damage from bludgeoning, slashing, and piercing damage while in a Mania. Add extra damage equal to the Mania Damage found on the level table. Mania lasts 1 minute.

## Furomania
When initiating [[#Mania]], roll a D4. A corresponding effect happens according to the *Furomania Roll Table*

> [!info]- Furomania Roll Table
>
> | Roll | Effect |
> | ---- | ------ |
> | 1 | Effect 1 |
> | 2 | Effect 2 |
> | 3 | Effect 3 |
> | 4 | Effect 4 |
> | 5 | Effect 5 |
> | 6 | Effect 6 |
