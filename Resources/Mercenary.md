## Furomantic Attack

Spend any number of available Mantic Charges to pump Furomantic energy into a creature when making a [[Fighter#Mantic Attack]]. On the start of the creature's next turn, it explodes with Furomantic power, damaging it and every creature within a 5ft radius for 1d6 per Mantic Charge expended.
