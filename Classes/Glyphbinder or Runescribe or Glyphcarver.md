## Glyph Binding #name-needed 
Gain the ability to draw runic circles on surfaces and power them using the [[Latent Radiation in Wastes]] while in [[The Wastes]] or outside. Each [[Glyphbinder or Runescribe or Glyphcarver]] can carve/draw [[Runes and Circles]] and then funnel [[Latent Radiation in Wastes]] into the [[Runes and Circles]] to create different effects.

At this level, learn 3 Tier 1 [[Runes and Circles]].
Gain 4 Tier 1 [[Runes and Circles]] "spell" slots.

## Glyph Binding Improvement #name-needed 
Improve your ability to draw and cast spells through your [[Runes and Circles]].
Tier 1 Runic Circles become more powerful: 
- Longer duration
- Greater power (increase a Tier 1 Runic Circle to a Tier 2 to increase its power - +1 to AC goes to +2 AC)
- Larger area of effect of Circle

At this level, learn 2 more Tier 1 [[Runes and Circles]].
At this level, learn 2 Tier 2 [[Runes and Circles]].
Gain 2 more Tier 1 [[Runes and Circles]] "rune" slots and 3 Tier 2 "rune" slots

## Glyph Binding Mastery #name-needed
Improve your ability to draw and cast spells through your [[Runes and Circles]].
Tier 1 and 2 Runic Circles become more powerful: 
- Longer duration
- Greater power (increase a Tier 1 Runic Circle to a Tier 3 to increase its power - +1 to AC goes to +3 AC)
- Larger area of effect of Circle

At this level, learn 1 more Tier 1 [[Runes and Circles]].
At this level, learn 2 more Tier 2 [[Runes and Circles]].
At this level, learn 2 Tier 3 [[Runes and Circles]].
Gain 1 more Tier 2 [[Runes and Circles]] "rune" slots and 3 Tier 3 "rune" slots
