## Potion Brewing #name-needed #todo
This character gains the ability to brew a number of [[Potions#Potions|potions]] according to their class table per rest (short and long). Each of these [[Potions]] goes inert after 8 hours and is therefore unusable.
Ingredients will need to be obtained from the environment and shops.

## Unstable Mixtures #name-needed #todo
This character gains the ability to create unstable concoctions from the potions they have brewed in past rests during combat to impact the world and creatures around them. As an action, an Alchemist can toss a reagent into a made potion to create something harmful and then throw the mixture at another creature - causing whatever effects that mixture has.
Reagents will need to be obtained from the environment and shops.
