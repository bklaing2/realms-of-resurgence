# Abilities
## Passive: Furostealth
Less detectable by low-power low-sentient horrors.
A level 5, hunters are unaffected by nonmagical difficult terrain

## Hide
Use an action to hide in shadows, rendering you virtually invisible. Attacks while hidden gain \
advantage, but you are no longer hidden. Can craft [[Arrows#Tier One|tier one arrows]].

## Sneak Attack
When you have advantage on an attack, add 3D6 to your damage roll once per turn. Can craft [[Arrows#Tier Two|tier two arrows]].

## Hunter's Mark
As a bonus action, mark a creature. Every attack against the creature gains advantage. When the creature dies the mark can be moved to another creature with a subsequent bonus action.

## Sneak Attack Upgrade #name-needed
When performing a [[#Sneak Attack]], add 5D6 instead of 3D6. Can craft [[Arrows#Tier Three|tier three arrows]].
