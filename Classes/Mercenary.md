# Abilities
## Passive: Fight or Flight
Once per short rest, can make an attack or dash (without provoking opp attack) as a bonus action
At level five you may use this twice per short rest

## Draining Hit #name-needed
Drain a bit of furomancy from an enemy on a successful hit. This will weaken the enemy (adds some extra damage).
You may use the stored furopoint to increase movement or as a reaction to get an attack of opportunity to move with them or keep them there

## Charged Hit #name-needed
Store furomancy when performing a [[#Draining Hit]]. Use the stored furomancy empower an attack. Also increases the range by 5 feet. You may choose to knock the enemy back.

## Healing Draining Hit #name-needed 
When performing a [[#Draining Hit name-needed]] choose to heal 1D6 instead of storing a furopoint.
Gains the ability to add a d6 to hit.

## Delayed Charged Attack #name-needed
Spend any number of stored furopoints to pump furomantic power into an enemy when making a [[#Charged Hit name-needed]]. On the start of the enemy's next turn, they explode with furomantic power, damaging the creature and every creature within a 5ft radius of them for 1D6 per furopoint used.


# Furopoints
