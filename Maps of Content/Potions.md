# Potions
**Potion of Healing (regular)** - 2 hours
- heals drinker 2d4+2 damage
**Potion of Resistance** - 1 hour
- can choose the type of elemental damage that the drinker takes half damage for
- lasts 5 minutes
**Potion of Speed** - 2 hours
- drinker adds 20 feet to their movement
- can make an additional weapon attack as a bonus action
- lasts 1 minute
**Potion of Chance - 15 minutes
- can re-roll a d20 and choose which roll to take
- lasts 8 hours
**Potion of Jumping** - 15 minutes
- drinker can triple their jump distance when getting a running start
- drinker can double their jump height when making a standing jump
- lasts 1 hour
**Potion of Dark Sight** - 30 minutes
- drinker can see further and in full darkness better
- furomantical essence has a slight glow
- lasts for 8 hours
