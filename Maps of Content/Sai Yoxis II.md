Sai Yoxis II is the second body in the [[Sai Yoxis System]]. It is not a planet, but rather a binary set of the twin planets [[Sai Yoxis II-A]] and [[Sai Yoxis II-B]], that trade their [[Neomancy]] and [[Antimancy]] back and forth to create [[Duomancy]].
