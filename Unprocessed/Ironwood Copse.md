[[Waste Environments]]
Small groupings of strong, tall trees unlike those around them. Their bark, the famous [[Ironwood Tree Bark]], can be used for many things as a powerful [[Components]], thus they are often inhabited by powerful creatures and sought after by others. Their bark and wood has a slight metallic coloring and smell to it.
