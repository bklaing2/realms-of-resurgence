[[The Wastes]]

People that - for whatever reason - did not mutate like all the rest. they woke up after [[The Cataclysm]] in the middle of the wreckage and just had incredible powers bestowed into them by the blast of [[Furomancy]].
because they are also "of the blast" the horrors see them as one of them. not attacking them at all (because the low power horrors can also sense how much power they have).

They became functionally immortal and just full of the latent energy that fills the world now. it pushed them to live and conquer [[The Wastes]] for themselves. many of them form symbiotic relationships with [[Low-sentience high-power monsters]] and sometimes even [[High-sentience high-power monsters]] to form very dangerous pairings to rule their corners of [[The Wastes]].
